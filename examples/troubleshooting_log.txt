# Cooling Pump Troubleshooting Log

## Incident 001

Date: 2026-02-14
Machine: Mill Line 3
Alarm: A12 low flow

The operator reported repeated low-flow alarms during morning startup. Inlet pressure was 205 kPa and outlet flow was 21 litres per minute.

The maintenance technician inspected the outlet valve and confirmed it was fully open.

The filter cartridge was removed and found to contain metal fines and coolant residue. The filter was replaced.

After replacement, outlet flow returned to 42 litres per minute and the alarm cleared.

Root cause: blocked filter cartridge.

## Incident 002

Date: 2026-03-03
Machine: Mill Line 2
Alarm: A18 high motor current

The pump motor current increased to 8.4 amps during continuous operation. Flow remained within normal range at 40 litres per minute.

The filter was inspected and was not blocked. Coolant viscosity was within specification.

The motor housing produced an irregular vibration. Bearing wear was suspected.

The pump was taken offline and the bearing assembly was replaced.

Root cause: bearing wear.

## Incident 003

Date: 2026-03-19
Machine: Mill Line 1
Alarm: A21 high coolant temperature

Coolant temperature rose to 51 degrees Celsius during peak load. Flow was 43 litres per minute and motor current was 6.9 amps.

Because flow and motor current were normal, the pump was not considered the primary fault.

The heat exchanger was inspected and fouling was found on the coolant side.

The heat exchanger was cleaned and coolant temperature returned to 39 degrees Celsius under standard load.

Root cause: heat exchanger fouling.

## Incident 004

Date: 2026-04-07
Machine: Mill Line 3
Alarm: A12 low flow

The controller recorded inlet pressure of 95 kPa and outlet flow of 18 litres per minute.

The filter cartridge was clean. The outlet valve was open.

The coolant reservoir was below the minimum fill line and air was present in the suction line.

The reservoir was refilled and the pump was primed.

Root cause: low coolant level and air in suction line.
